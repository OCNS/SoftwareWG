Members

The WG is open to everyone: without any formal on-boarding process or requirements (academic or otherwise).

Code of Conduct⋆

All interactions on any channels of the WG must adhere to the Code of Conduct.

Enforcement⋆

If you experience or become aware of any behaviour that does not adhere to the Code of Conduct, please assume good faith and point the involved parties to the Code of Conduct to begin with. It is more likely that they are unaware of their harmful behaviour rather than that they intentionally engage in it. Should difficulties arise, please report the situation to the co-chairs (e-mail addresses in the next section).
